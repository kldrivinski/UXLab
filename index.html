About Us

Shitty Roads is a navigation system here to clear your path. Whether you're dealing with chronic potholes or never-ending traffic, we're here to get you where you need to be (with your car still intact).

Shitty roads was started to help bring relief to your commute. We use the latest traffic updates to tell you where you need to be. We care about your time and your sanity and our navigation can help save both.

Our team

Our team consists of Sydney, Kelsey, Dequan and Colin. We're a group of developers and designers who wonder if the state budget for roads has gotten lost in one of it's giant potholes.
